Privacy Policy

VEXORA ("VEXORA", "we", "our", or "us") operates an international payment and financial services platform. We are committed to protecting your personal data and handling it in a transparent and secure manner.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your personal data when you access or use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of data:

1.1 Personal Identification Information

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Nationality
  • Government-issued identification documents (for KYC purposes)
  • Contact information (email address, phone number)

1.2 Financial & Transaction Information

  • Payment transaction details
  • Wallet or account identifiers
  • Payment method information (processed via secure third-party providers)

1.3 Technical & Usage Data

  • IP address
  • Device type, operating system, and browser
  • Log data, timestamps, and usage behavior

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a contract
  • Compliance with legal obligations (e.g., AML, KYC, financial regulations)
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Your explicit consent, where required by law

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the VEXORA platform
  • Process payments and financial transactions
  • Conduct identity verification (KYC) and fraud prevention
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improve security, performance, and user experience
  • Communicate service-related notices and updates

4. KYC, AML, and Compliance Monitoring

To comply with applicable financial regulations, we may:

  • Verify your identity through KYC procedures
  • Conduct ongoing monitoring of transactions
  • Share data with compliance service providers and regulators when legally required

Failure to complete KYC verification may result in restricted or suspended access to services.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Identity verification and fraud prevention providers
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure partners
  • Legal or regulatory authorities as required by law

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. International Data Transfers

VEXORA operates globally. Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence.

Where required by law (including GDPR), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequate data protection frameworks
  • Secure technical and organizational measures

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Meet legal, accounting, and regulatory obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Retention periods may vary depending on legal requirements.

8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

Requests may be submitted via the contact details below.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
