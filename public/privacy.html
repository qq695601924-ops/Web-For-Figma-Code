Privacy Policy

At Graysville Trading Limited ("we," "us," or "our"), we are deeply committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect your information when you use the LendMax app ("the App").

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the App. For any questions or concerns, contact us at info@lendmaxng.com.

1. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the LendMax app and any associated services provided by Graysville Trading Limited. It covers data collection, usage, sharing, and storage practices.

2. Categories of Personal Data Collected

We obtain specific categories of personal data with your knowledge and consent, either provided directly by you or gathered automatically from your device. This information is essential for evaluating loan eligibility, improving the quality of our services, and meeting legal and regulatory requirements.

2.1 Identity and Contact Information

Full legal name, date of birth, gender, and marital status

Educational background and residential address

Phone number, email address, and messaging platform identifiers (e.g., WhatsApp)

Emergency contact details, including full name, relationship, and mobile number

2.2 Employment and Financial Information

Employment status, job type, industry, and professional experience

Monthly income details and payment frequency

Bank account information, bank name, and Bank Verification Number (BVN)

2.3 Device and Technical Information

Device type, operating system version, and unique identifiers (e.g., Android ID)

IP address, language settings, time zone, and screen resolution

Mobile network details, signal strength, battery status, RAM, and storage capacity

2.4 Behavioral and Transactional Data

Loan application records and submission details

Credit assessment results and repayment history

In-app activities, user behavior, and engagement statistics

2.5 Third-Party and Social Data

Information obtained from credit bureaus, KYC providers, and public databases

Social metadata (where legally permitted), such as profile verification or limited account insights

3.  Sensitive Permissions and Information Access

At LendMax, we adhere to the principles of data minimization, transparency, and explicit user consent. We only request device permissions when they are strictly necessary to provide or enhance our services. Each permission is explained in advance and requires your active approval.

3.1 Permissions We Do Not Request

To safeguard your privacy and avoid unnecessary data collection, LendMax does not request or process the following categories of data:

Precise GPS location

Photos, files, or media library access

Microphone or voice recordings

Contacts, call history, or call logs

Calendar events, browser history, or installed apps list

Biometric data (except when expressly authorized for e-KYC)

These data types are not required for service delivery and will never be collected.

3.2 Permissions That May Be Requested (With Consent)

In certain cases, and only with your prior approval, we may request access to specific data types to enable essential features:

SMS Permission

Objective: To enhance credit risk assessment through limited financial SMS analysis.

Filtering condition:

Including sender information, timestamps and financial-related keywords sms, such as Leverage, Syndication, Repossession, Collateralized, Factoring, Nonperforming, Recovery, Covenant Breach, Write-Off, Capitalization, Forfeiture, credit, wallet, naira, money, kash, cash, moni, lend, loan, repay, bank, trans, disburse, ngn, acc, bal, debit, debt, borrow, risk, deposite, installment, amt, amount, limit, interest, rate, ngn, fund, quot, ₦, default, due, settle, expire, mature, collect.

Excluding messages from personal contacts (e.g. +234 number) are not included. Excluding non-financial content (such as shopping, education, entertainment, healthcare). Excluding notifications of telecommunications services (e.g., MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9Mobile).

Secure transmission and storage: All financial-related SMS data is transmitted to our server (https://h5.lendmaxng.com) using a secure encryption protocol. Sensitive information will be obfuscated in real time to ensure that no original personal identity information is leaked during transmission or storage.

Strict data confidentiality: SMS data related to finance will never be shared with any third party or external institution. The risk management system only outputs assessment results that cannot be traced back to the original content.

Data lifecycle management: The system will anonymize the data and retain it for up to 120 days to assist in fraud investigations and dispute resolution. After that, it will be permanently deleted. If you wish to delete SMS messages, please contact our online customer service within the app. We will complete the deletion within three business days.

Camera Access

We may request camera access solely for secure, limited functions:

Identity verification: Capturing ID documents and photos for KYC compliance

Biometric authentication: Facial recognition to prevent fraud or impersonation

Security: Images are encrypted during transmission and storage, and securely protected on our servers.

Approximate Location

If you grant permission, we may access approximate (non-precise) location data to:

Validate consistency between provided and actual region

Detect unusual patterns for fraud prevention

Location access is optional, non-real-time, and can be disabled anytime.

Advertising Identifier (AD_ID)

We may use your device’s advertising identifier strictly for:

Campaign attribution (measuring effectiveness of ads via Google, Facebook, etc.)

Analytics on acquisition quality and performance

AD_ID is not linked to personal identifiers, and you may reset or disable it anytime through device settings.

Network Connectivity

We may check your device’s network status to:

Ensure app features function properly when online

Detect potentially risky behaviors (e.g., VPN or proxy usage)

We do not collect browsing history, installed apps, or unrelated network data.

3.3 SDK Usage

LendMax integrates the AppsFlyer SDK (officially listed on Google Play SDK Console), used exclusively for attribution and analytics.

Data collected by AppsFlyer SDK may include:

Advertising identifier (Google AD_ID)

Approximate location data

In-app behavior (page views, button clicks)

Performance metrics (crash logs, diagnostics)

Purpose of SDK integration:

Track app installations and activations

Monitor session usage

Record in-app events (e.g., form submissions, transactions)

Assess user quality and advertising ROI

All SDK data is transmitted securely with end-to-end encryption. We do not use SDK to collect precise GPS location, messages, contacts, or other sensitive content.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable laws and regulations. Specifically:

Account Information: Retained for the duration of your account's active status and up to 5 years after account closure to comply with legal, regulatory, or audit requirements.

Loan Application Data: Retained for 5 years after the loan is fully repaid or the application is withdrawn, in compliance with financial regulations.

Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years to meet regulatory and tax obligations.

Communication Data: Emails, support requests, and other communications are retained for 2 years for quality assurance and dispute resolution purposes.

After these retention periods, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized unless otherwise required by law.
